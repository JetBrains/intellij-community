Did you know that IntelliJ IDEA lets you jump from your checkin comment to an issue in tracker?
All you have to do is to define issue navigation patterns in the Settings | Version Control dialog:

When you commit a changelist, and type checkin comment, make sure it matches one of your issue navigation patterns. Later, when you browse through the changelists in the repository, such comments turn to hyperlinks:

Clicking this hyperlink brings you directly to an issue in your task tracker.
