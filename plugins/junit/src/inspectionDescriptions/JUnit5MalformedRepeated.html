Reports problems with JUnit 5 @RepeatedTest:
  • @Test and @RepeatedTest on the same method (even though it is allowed, they have no additional effect together).
  • RepetitionInfo parameter is used in @BeforeAll/@AfterAll methods (it is not injected in these methods).
  • RepetitionInfo parameter is used in @BeforeEach/@AfterEach methods in classes with @Test methods (in this case, it won't be injected for @Test methods).

New in 2017.2
