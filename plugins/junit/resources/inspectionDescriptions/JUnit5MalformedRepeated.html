Reports the following problems with JUnit 5 @RepeatedTest:

  • @Test and @RepeatedTest are used on the same method. Even though it is allowed to use them, they have no additional effect together.
  • RepetitionInfo parameter is used in the @BeforeAll and @AfterAll methods. The parameter is not injected in these methods.
  • RepetitionInfo parameter is used in the @BeforeEach and @AfterEach methods in classes with @Test methods. In this case, the parameter won't be injected in @Test methods.
