Reports the following light service problems:
  • A class annotated with the @com.intellij.openapi.components.Service annotation is not final.
  • A service having a constructor that takes a com.intellij.openapi.project.Project parameter is not annotated as project-level service.
  • A service annotated as an application-level service does not have a no-arg constructor, nor a constructor that takeskotlinx.coroutines.CoroutineScope.
  • Mismatch in retrieving a service: getting a project-level service as an application-level service and vice versa.

Please see Light Services for further reference.
