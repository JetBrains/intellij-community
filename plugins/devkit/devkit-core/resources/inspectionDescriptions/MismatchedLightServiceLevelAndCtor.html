Reports mismatches between light service levels and constructors.

The following problems are reported:

  • A light service class has a constructor with one parameter of type com.intellij.openapi.project.Project is not annotated as project-level service.
  • A light service class annotated as an application-level service does not have a no-arg constructor, nor a constructor with one parameter of type kotlinx.coroutines.CoroutineScope.
