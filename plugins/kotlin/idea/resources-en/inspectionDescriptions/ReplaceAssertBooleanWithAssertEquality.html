Reports assert boolean function calls replaceable with assert equality function.

assertTrue() and assertFalse() have more specialized alternative to compare booleans.
assertEquals(), assertSame() and their paired negating ones (-Not-) provide more informative messages on failure.

The inspection is paired with 'Replace assert boolean with assert equality' quick-fix.

Example:


  assertTrue(a == b)

After the quick-fix is applied:


  assertEquals(a, b)
