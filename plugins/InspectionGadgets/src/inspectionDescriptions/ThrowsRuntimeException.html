Reports declarations of unchecked exceptions (java.lang.RuntimeException and its subclasses) in the throws clause of a method. Declaration of unchecked exceptions are not required and may be deleted or moved to a Javadoc @throws tag.

Example:

  public class InvalidDataException extends RuntimeException {}

  class TextEditor {
    void readSettings() throws InvalidDataException {} // warning: Unchecked exception 'InvalidDataException' declared in 'throws' clause
  }
