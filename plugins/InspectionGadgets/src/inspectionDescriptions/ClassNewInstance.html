Reports any calls to java.lang.Class.newInstance().

The newInstance method propagates any exception thrown by the no-arg constructor, including a checked exceptions. Use of this method effectively bypasses the compile-time exception checking that would otherwise be performed by the compiler.

The quick-fix replaces such a method call with a call to the java.lang.reflect.Constructor.newInstance() method. This method avoids this problem by wrapping any exception thrown by the constructor in a (checked) java.lang.reflect.InvocationTargetException.

Example:


  clazz.newInstance()

After the quick-fix is applied:


  clazz.getConstructor().newInstance();
