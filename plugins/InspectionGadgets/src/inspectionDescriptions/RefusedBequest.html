Reports any methods which override concrete methods, but which do not call that method as super. This is also known as a refused bequest and such methods may represent a failure of abstraction which can lead to hard-to-trace bugs. Methods overridden from java.lang.Object except for clone() are not reported by this inspection. A clone() method is expected to call its super, which will automatically return an object of the correct type.
