Reports JUnit 4+ test methods whose names are either too short, too long, or do not follow the specified regular expression pattern. When this inspection is enabled, the Instance method naming convention inspection will ignore JUnit 4+ test methods automatically.

Use the fields below to specify minimum length, maximum length and regular expression expected for JUnit 4+ test method names. Specify 0 to not check the length of names. Regular expressions are in standard java.util.regex format.
